Identity: The New Perimeter in Cybersecurity

March 9, 2025

Yesterday, we covered the foundations of cybersecurity. Today, let’s zoom in on a critical shift: identity is the new perimeter. As a security architect, I’ve watched the old castle-and-moat model—firewalls guarding a trusted inside—fade away. In a cloud-first world, it’s all about *who* has access.

1. Perimeters Dissolved: Cloud services, remote work, and BYOD mean your data lives everywhere. A VPN isn’t enough—identity controls are the frontline now.

2. Authentication is King: Passwords alone don’t cut it. Multi-factor authentication (MFA), biometrics, or tokens—strong identity verification stops unauthorized entry cold.

3. Authorization Matters Too: It’s not just about getting in—it’s about what you can do. Granular policies ensure users touch only what they need, nothing more.

Identity isn’t an afterthought; it’s the core of modern security. Tomorrow, I’ll explore how this plays out with access management in the cloud. Keep reading!
