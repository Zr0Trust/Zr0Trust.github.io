Cybersecurity Foundations: The Bedrock of Protection

March 8, 2025

Cybersecurity isn’t just a buzzword—it’s the backbone of our digital world. With over six years in security, I’ve seen how the stakes keep rising. Whether it’s cloud workloads or on-prem systems, the fundamentals remain the same. Let’s kick off this series by laying the groundwork.

1. Know Your Assets: You can’t protect what you don’t understand. Start by cataloging your systems—servers, apps, data stores. Visibility is step one; without it, you’re blind to risks.

2. Threats Are Everywhere: Assume compromise. External hackers, insider threats, even misconfigurations—danger lurks in every corner. Cybersecurity begins with accepting that no system is inherently safe.

3. Defense in Depth: One layer isn’t enough. Firewalls, encryption, access controls—stack them up. If one fails, another catches the slack. It’s not paranoia; it’s pragmatism.

These basics set the stage for everything else. Tomorrow, I’ll dive into why identity is the new perimeter in this ever-shifting landscape. Stay tuned!
