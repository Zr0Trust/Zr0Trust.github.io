Access Management: Controlling the Keys to Your Kingdom

March 10, 2025

Building on yesterday’s focus on identity, let’s talk access management—the gatekeeper of your digital kingdom. With six years securing systems, I’ve learned that who you let in and what they can touch defines your cybersecurity posture.

1. Principle of Least Privilege: Give users and services only the access they need—nothing extra. It’s simple: less permission, less risk.

2. Dynamic Access: Static credentials are a liability. Temporary, role-based access—think short-lived tokens—keeps things tight and adaptable.

3. Audit Everything: You can’t manage what you can’t see. Logging access attempts and actions lets you spot trouble before it escalates.

Access management is where theory meets practice, especially in the cloud. Tomorrow, I’ll tie this into AWS with a deep dive on IAM—don’t miss it!
